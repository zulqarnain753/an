Swiss National Park

This app shows the hiking trails for the Swiss National Park, the largest and most highly protected area in Switzerland.

Disclaimer

This is a non-commercial, demo application made at the Esri R&D Center Zurich for visualizing hiking trails in the Swiss National Park. It is built on top of ArcGIS API for JavaScript version 4.20.

Credits

Data for the hiking trails was kindly provided by the Swiss National Park.
